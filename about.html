Message

Our Founder's Message

Engineering has always been more than just a profession for me — it has been a way of thinking, building, and creating impact.

From my early days as a Formula Student engineer and Chassis Lead, I learned the discipline of design, precision, and teamwork. Those experiences shaped my passion for engineering and pushed me to transform ideas into reality. With that foundation, I started ASB Engineering Services with a clear goal — to deliver world-class engineering and manufacturing solutions that solve real industry challenges.

Our journey began with small projects, but with consistency and commitment, we have earned the trust of leading organizations, including TATA and Raychem RPG. Today, ASB Engineering Services provides a wide spectrum of solutions — from sheet metal fabrication and CNC machining to custom assemblies and industrial HVAC products. What sets us apart is our ability to combine technical depth, precision-driven processes, and customer-focused execution.

At ASB, we believe that engineering must go beyond production — it must create value, efficiency, and progress. Every component we deliver reflects our core values: accuracy, reliability, and innovation.

But this is just the beginning. My vision is to build ASB into a globally recognized engineering powerhouse, expanding our capabilities, scaling into new industries, and setting benchmarks in quality and innovation. Beyond business, I aim to inspire young engineers and ambitious entrepreneurs, empowering them to shape their own futures through engineering and enterprise.

I welcome you to explore ASB Engineering Services and partner with us in building solutions that last, products that perform, and innovations that inspire.

– Aniket Barkade
