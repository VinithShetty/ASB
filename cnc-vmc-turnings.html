High-Precision CNC & VMC Machined Components

ASB Engineering Services offers precision CNC and VMC machining services for high-performance components across industries. We specialize in complex geometries, tight tolerances, and production consistency for small to high-volume requirements.

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art CNC turning centers, vertical machining centers (VMC), and multi-axis machines capable of producing intricate parts with superior accuracy. From one-off prototypes to mass production, we ensure every component meets strict dimensional and quality standards. We machine a wide range of ferrous and non-ferrous metals, supported by in-house CAD/CAM programming, inspection, and fast project turnaround.

Materials

  • Stainless Steel (304, 316, 410, 420)
  • Carbon Steel (EN8, EN19, EN24)
  • Mild Steel (IS2062, CRCA)
  • Aluminium (6061, 6082, ADC12)
  • Brass, Bronze, Copper Alloys
  • Cast Iron (FG200, FG260, SG Iron)

Machinery Equipment

  • CNC Turning Centers – 2-Axis, 3-Axis
  • Vertical Machining Centers (VMC) – 3-Axis, 4-Axis & 5-Axis
  • Tool Room Lathes with DRO
  • Hydraulic & Pneumatic Fixtures
  • CNC Lathe with Live Tooling
  • Digital Calipers, Bore Gauges, Height Gauges

Machining Capabilities

  • OD Turning & ID Boring
  • Thread Cutting (Internal & External)
  • Grooving, Knurling, Chamfering
  • Face Milling, Slotting, Drilling, Tapping
  • Surface Finishing up to Ra 0.4 µm
  • Batch Sizes from 1 to 10,000+ parts
