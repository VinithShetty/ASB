what we do

Our Manufacturing Expertise

ASB Engineering Services specializes in low-to-medium volume, high-mix contract manufacturing, with a focus on industrial products, mechanical components, and custom assemblies. Our core expertise lies in sheet metal fabrication, precision machining, and component integration, enabling us to serve diverse industries with durable, high-performance solutions. Backed by deep vertical integration, we offer competitive pricing, stringent quality control, and exceptional production flexibility.

With years of combined experience and a dynamic team of skilled Indian engineers and manufacturing experts, ASB delivers end-to-end solutions for mechanical, electromechanical, and fabricated assemblies. From CNC machining, laser cutting, and press components to custom sheet metal and welded structures, we seamlessly integrate in-house capabilities for efficient and reliable production. Our turnkey manufacturing approach simplifies the process, ensuring smooth execution from concept to final product delivery.
