Comprehensive Pipe & Tube Fabrication

At ASB Engineering Services, we deliver end-to-end solutions for the manufacturing of tube and pipe components, assemblies, and sub-assemblies. Backed by deep expertise in DFM (Design for Manufacturing), we ensure every part meets precise specifications and production timelines. Our proven capabilities help clients achieve efficient, high-quality results in tube and pipe fabrication.

We specialize in contract manufacturing solutions that include comprehensive pipe and tube fabrication, finishing, and complete assembly services. From material sourcing and custom tooling to precision cutting, bending, welding, and stamping, we handle every stage of production. Our expertise also extends to fastener integration, hardware installation, and seamless assembly with sheet metal, plastics, mechanical, and electronic components.

our expertise

Precision Services

From bespoke tube bending to turnkey assemblies — engineered for performance and manufacturability.

Precision Pipe Bending

Advanced CNC pipe bending capabilities for complex geometries, maintaining precise tolerances and smooth surface finish for all pipe materials.

Tube Cutting & Notching

Precision cutting and notching services for tubes and pipes, including end forming, chamfering, and custom profile cutting for perfect fit assemblies.

Welding & Joining

Expert welding services including TIG, MIG, and specialized welding processes for stainless steel, aluminum, and carbon steel pipes and tubes.

Surface Treatment

Comprehensive surface finishing including pickling, passivation, electropolishing, and coating applications for corrosion resistance and aesthetics.

Custom Fabrication

Complete custom pipe and tube assemblies, including flanging, threading, and complex multi-bend configurations for specialized applications.

Quality Testing

Non-destructive testing, pressure testing, and dimensional verification to ensure all pipe and tube products meet specifications and safety standards.
